Overview

Aastha is a fully gated NA plotted layout at Tembhode, Palghar. Fully leveled and serviced with 9M and 12M internal RCC roads, RCC stormwater gutters, perimeter fencing and street lighting throughout, the site has direct connectivity to the Manor-Mahim State Highway in one of Palghar's fastest-growing corridors.

Aastha is a venture involving partners of Om Shanti N R Construction. The land is being released for sale under a single-owner gated layout, with NA title, all possession certificates and statutory documentation in place, to individual plot owners as well as to builders and developers acquiring larger parcels. Every transaction is backed by transparent, title-clear paperwork and a Vastu-aligned road grid.

Whether you are an individual buyer planning a private home or weekend residence, or a developer looking for cleared, infrastructure-ready land in a high-growth Palghar pocket, Aastha is positioned as a transparent, compliance-first plotted opportunity, backed by 30+ years of construction and land experience from the partners of Om Shanti N R Construction.

Frequently Asked Questions
Are the Aastha plots NA-converted? +
Yes. Aastha is laid out as a Non-Agricultural (NA) plotted development. The order & documentation are part of the buyer kit shared during site visit. We strongly recommend personally verifying the latest 7/12 extract, NA order and architect's sanctioned plan before booking.
What approvals are in place at the time of launch? +
The architect's plotting layout (T.I.L.R) is in place. Statutory approvals (Collector NA, Talathi mutation entries, Town Planning sanction) and MahaRERA registration where applicable are progressed in parallel and shared transparently with each interested buyer.
When can I start construction on my plot? +
Construction can be initiated after final possession is handed over and the buyer obtains the appropriate building permissions on their plot. Aastha's gated infrastructure (roads, lighting, gutters, fencing) is delivered to plot edge so construction starts smoothly.
Are home/plot loans available? +
Yes. Most leading public-sector and private banks finance NA-converted plotted layouts with clear title. We will share the pre-approved bank list during your enquiry call. Final loan eligibility is determined by the bank based on individual profile.
What is the typical payment plan? +
Aastha uses a milestone-linked payment schedule — with a booking amount, agreement-stage payment and milestone-linked instalments tied to layout-level infrastructure progress (roads, gutters, lighting, fencing). The full schedule is shared in writing along with the price sheet.
What is included in the plot price? +
The plot price includes the NA-converted land parcel, share in the gated layout's common infrastructure (RCC roads, RCC gutters, street lighting, outer fencing, boundary), and applicable layout-level approvals. GST, registration, stamp duty and individual building-construction costs are over and above the plot price.
Can I visit the site before booking? +
Absolutely — site visits are encouraged and accompanied by our team. WhatsApp us on +91 82628 85023 to schedule a slot, share your preferred time and we'll arrange a convenient visit including a walk-through of the layout boundary, sample plot positions and infrastructure works.
What is the possession timeline? +
Plotted possession depends on phase release and statutory milestones. Indicative timelines are shared in writing at the time of booking, along with infrastructure-completion milestones. Aastha follows a phased delivery to ensure each plot is handed over with completed road, lighting, gutter and fencing works at the boundary.
